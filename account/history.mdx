Griilll keeps a full record of every round you've played. From the History screen, you can see each opponent, the stake you wagered, the outcome, and how your chip balance changed — all sorted by most recent first.

## Opening your history

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your profile">
    Tap the **Profile icon** in the bottom-right corner of the home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap History">
    Tap **"History"** to open your full round log.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse your rounds">
    You'll see a list of every round you've played, with the most recent at the top.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What each history entry shows

Every entry in your history includes:

- **Date and time** of the round
- **Opponent's username**
- **Stake** — the number of chips wagered
- **Outcome** — Win, Loss, Draw, or Cancelled
- **Net chip change** — for example, +100 or −50

## Stats summary

At the top of the History screen, Griilll displays your all-time stats at a glance:

- **Total rounds played**
- **Win rate** (%)
- **Biggest single win**
- **Biggest single loss**
- **Total chips won / lost**

## Filtering your history

<Accordion title="Filter and search your round history">
  Tap the **filter icon** in the top-right corner of the History screen to narrow down your results. You can filter to show:

  - Only wins
  - Only losses
  - Rounds against a specific opponent (search by username)
  - A custom date range

  Clear the filter at any time to return to your full history.
</Accordion>

<Note>
  Your history is stored on Griilll's servers and syncs to any device where you're logged in to your account.
</Note>

<Tip>
  After each session, take a moment to review your history. Looking back at your stakes and outcomes can help you spot patterns in how you bet and sharpen your strategy for next time.
</Tip>
