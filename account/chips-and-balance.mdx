Chips are the currency of Griilll. Every wager costs chips; every win earns them. Your chip balance is shown at the top of the home screen at all times and updates in real time after every round.

## How chips work

- Chips are held in your Griilll account, not on your device
- When you win a round, chips move from your opponent's balance to yours instantly
- When you lose, the wagered chips move to your opponent
- You can never go below zero — the app prevents you from wagering chips you don't have

## Starter chips

<Note>
  Every new account receives 500 free starter chips. These chips work identically to purchased chips and can be wagered normally.
</Note>

## Viewing your balance

Your current chip balance is always visible in the **top-right corner of the home screen**, displayed as a chip icon alongside your total. You never have to dig through menus to check where you stand.

During stake negotiation — when you and your opponent are agreeing on how many chips to put up — your balance appears **below the stake slider** so you can see exactly how much you have available before confirming the wager.

## Balance safety

<Note>
  Your balance is stored on Griilll's cloud servers and is tied to your account, not your device. If you switch phones or reinstall the app, your full balance is restored as soon as you log in.
</Note>
