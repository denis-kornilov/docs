Got questions about Griilll? This page covers everything from downloading the app to understanding your chip balance. If you don't find your answer here, reach out via the in-app Help menu.

<Accordion title="Is Griilll free to download?">
  Yes. Griilll is free to download on iOS and Android. New accounts receive 500 free starter chips. Additional chips can be purchased inside the app.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I need an internet connection to play?">
  No internet connection is required once you're in a game. Griilll uses local wireless (Bluetooth and local network) between two devices. You do need an internet connection to log in, top up chips, or view your history, but gameplay itself is fully offline.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I play against someone far away?">
  No. Griilll is designed for two players who are physically close together. Both phones must be within roughly 30 cm to 1 m of each other to pair. Remote play is not supported.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What devices are supported?">
  Griilll is available on iPhones running iOS 15 or later and Android phones running Android 10 or later. Both players do not need to have the same type of device — an iPhone can play against an Android phone.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to my chips if I uninstall the app?">
  Your chip balance is stored in your Griilll account, not on your device. Uninstalling the app does not affect your balance. Log back in after reinstalling and your chips will be there.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I have multiple accounts?">
  One account per person is permitted. Griilll uses phone number verification to prevent duplicate accounts.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a limit on how many chips I can win in a day?">
  There is no daily winning cap. You can play as many rounds as you and your opponent agree to.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
  Go to **Profile → Settings → Delete Account**. This permanently removes your account and balance.

  <Warning>
    Chip balances cannot be refunded on account deletion. Make sure you want to permanently close your account before proceeding.
  </Warning>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
  Tap **Profile → Help** inside the app, or email [support@griilll.com](mailto:support@griilll.com). Support responds within 24 hours on business days.
</Accordion>
