When a Griilll round ends, the result screen appears on both phones simultaneously. The winner sees a celebration screen; the loser sees how many chips they lost. Chips move from the loser's balance to the winner's balance in real time — no confirmation required, no waiting.

## The result screen

Both players see the outcome at exactly the same moment:

- **If you won:** A celebration screen displays **"You Won! +X chips"** in large text, followed by your new balance directly below.
- **If you lost:** The screen shows **"You Lost — X chips"** along with your updated (lower) balance beneath it.
- **Both players** also see a summary of the round, including the stake amount and both players' post-round balances, before the rematch offer appears.

## Chip transfer

Chip transfer is **instant and automatic**. The moment the result is determined, chips leave the loser's balance and land in the winner's. There's no transfer screen to dismiss and no confirmation tap needed — if you win 100 chips, your balance reads 100 higher before you've even lifted your finger.

Your updated balance appears on the result screen and persists across all future rounds and sessions.

## What if it's a draw?

<Accordion title="The round ended in a tie — what happens to my chips?">
  In the rare event of a true tie, **no chips move**. Both balances stay exactly as they were before the round. Griilll immediately offers both players a free rematch at the same stake so you can break the deadlock — no renegotiation, no delay, just tap Rematch and go again.
</Accordion>

## Running out of chips

<Note>
  If your balance hits zero, you won't be able to start or accept new rounds until you add more chips. Head to [Top Up](/account/top-up) to reload your balance and get back in the game.
</Note>
