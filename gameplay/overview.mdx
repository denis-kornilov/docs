Every Griilll game is a single round between two players. There's no board, no turns — both players act at the same moment, the app determines the winner in real time, and chips move instantly.

## Round structure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stake negotiation">
    One player proposes a chip amount; the other accepts or counters. Play doesn't begin until both players confirm the same stake.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Countdown">
    Once the stake is locked, a 3-second on-screen countdown appears on both phones simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Simultaneous tap">
    Both players tap at zero. Your tap is recorded the instant your finger hits the screen — no delays, no turn order.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Result">
    The winner is declared immediately. Chips transfer from the loser's balance to the winner's balance in real time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rematch offer">
    Both players see Rematch and Leave buttons. Agree to a rematch and a new round kicks off at the same stake — no renegotiation needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The game mechanic

Griilll uses a provably fair algorithm to determine the outcome of every round. Both players tap simultaneously and the result is resolved the instant the countdown hits zero — think of it as a digital coin flip where reflexes and chance collide. The algorithm is seeded independently for each round and can be audited after the fact, so neither player can predict or manipulate the outcome.

The beauty of Griilll is its simplicity: no complex strategy, no waiting for turns — just two players, one moment, one winner.

## Round duration

From stake confirmation to final result, a typical Griilll round takes **15–30 seconds**. The negotiation phase is the only variable — once both players confirm, the countdown, tap, and result happen in under ten seconds.

<Note>
  Both players must stay connected throughout the round. If one player's connection drops mid-round, the round is cancelled automatically and **no chips move**. Both players return to the pre-round screen with their balances unchanged.
</Note>
