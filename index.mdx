## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Griilll on both phones">
    Both players launch the app. No account linking or invitation codes needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bring phones close together">
    Hold the two devices within about 30 cm of each other. Griilll detects the nearby device automatically using local wireless.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree on the stakes">
    One player proposes a chip amount to wager. The other accepts. The round begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play the round">
    Both players take their action. The result is instant — chips transfer to the winner right away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  No internet connection is required during a game. Griilll uses local device-to-device communication, so you can play anywhere — on a train, at a cafe, or at home.
</Tip>
