When your chip balance runs low, you can top up directly inside the Griilll app. Chip packs are available in several sizes and are processed through your device's native payment system (Apple Pay, Google Pay, or saved card).

## How to top up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your balance">
    Open Griilll and tap your balance in the top-right corner of the home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Get Chips">
    Tap **"Get Chips"** to open the chip pack selection screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a chip pack">
    Browse the available packs and tap the one you want to purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the purchase">
    Authenticate with Face ID, fingerprint, or your device PIN to approve the payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your chips">
    Your chips appear in your balance within seconds — you're ready to play.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Chip packs

Choose the pack that fits your session. Prices shown in the app vary by region.

| Pack | Chips |
|---|---|
| Starter Pack | 1,000 chips |
| Pro Pack | 5,000 chips |
| High Roller Pack | 25,000 chips |

## Payment methods

Griilll supports **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay** for fast, secure checkout. On some devices you can also use a saved credit or debit card. All payments are handled entirely by your device's native payment infrastructure — Griilll never stores your card details.

<Note>
  All purchases are final. Chips are non-refundable once they have been added to your balance.
</Note>

<Warning>
  Play responsibly. Only top up an amount you're comfortable wagering — chip packs cannot be reversed once purchased.
</Warning>

## Didn't receive your chips?

<Accordion title="Troubleshoot a missing chip delivery">
  Wait 60 seconds and pull down to refresh the app. Chip delivery is usually instant, but occasional network delays can add a brief lag.

  If your chips still haven't appeared after refreshing, check your email for a purchase receipt to confirm the payment completed on your device's payment platform.

  If the payment went through but the chips are still missing from your balance, contact **support@griilll.com** and attach your purchase receipt. The support team will reconcile your account manually.
</Accordion>
