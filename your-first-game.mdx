This guide walks you through a complete Griilll game from start to finish. By the end you'll have connected to an opponent, placed a bet, played a round, and seen chips land in the winner's balance. The whole thing takes about three minutes — follow along and you'll know exactly what to expect every step of the way.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and open Griilll">
    Both players install Griilll from the App Store or Google Play and open the app. You'll each be prompted to create a free account — this takes about 30 seconds with just an email address or phone number. Once you're in, you'll land on your home screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim your starter chips">
    New accounts receive free starter chips automatically the first time you open the app. You'll see a short animation confirming the chips have landed in your balance. No purchase needed — you're ready to play straight away.

    <Note>
      Starter chips are free-to-play chips designed to help you learn the game without any financial commitment. If you enjoy Griilll and want to play with purchased chips, you can top up your balance from the **Wallet** screen at any time. Free starter chips and purchased chips behave identically in-game — the only difference is where they came from.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to your opponent">
    Sit or stand next to the person you want to play. Both of you tap **Find Opponent**, then bring your phones within 30 cm of each other. When you see your opponent's username appear, tap **Confirm**.

    For a detailed breakdown of the connection process, see the [Connect Phones](/how-to-connect) guide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the stake">
    Once connected, you'll reach the stake-setting screen. One player proposes a chip amount — for example, **50 chips**. The other player can either:

    - **Accept** — the stake is locked in and the round begins
    - **Counter** — propose a different amount; the first player then accepts or counters again

    Both players must agree on the same stake before the round starts. There's no time limit on negotiation, so take a moment to agree on something you're both comfortable with.

    <Tip>
      For your very first game, start with a small stake — 10 or 25 chips is plenty. This gives you a chance to get a feel for the timing and mechanics before you put more chips on the line.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Play the round">
    When the stake is confirmed, a countdown screen appears on both phones. Watch the countdown and — when it hits zero — tap your action simultaneously with your opponent. Both phones register your inputs at the same instant and determine the outcome locally, with no server involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See the result">
    The result screen appears on both phones at the same time. It shows:

    - **Who won** — the winner's username is highlighted
    - **Chips transferred** — the exact number of chips that moved from loser to winner
    - **Updated balances** — both players see their new totals immediately

    Chip balances update instantly. There's no delay, no withdrawal process, and no confirmation step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rematch or leave">
    After the result, you have two options:

    - **Rematch** — play another round with the same stake. Both players must confirm to start again.
    - **Leave** — disconnect and return to the home screen. Your updated balance is saved automatically.

    Either player can choose to leave at any time after a round completes. Mid-round disconnection is handled according to the forfeit rules in the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Next?

Now that you've played your first game, you know the full Griilll loop. From here you can explore the **Wallet** screen to top up with purchased chips, check the **History** tab to review past results, or — better yet — challenge someone new.
